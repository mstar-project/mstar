Leaves fall to the ground,
A rustling symphony of sound,
Autumn's gentle hush.

Crimson and gold leaves,
Dance in the crisp autumn breeze,
Nature's final show.

Leaves crunch underfoot,
A carpet of orange and red,
Autumn's fleeting beauty.

Leaves whisper secrets,
Of summer's warmth and life's end,
Autumn's bittersweet song.

Leaves drift through the air,
A kaleidoscope of color,
Autumn's vibrant art.
