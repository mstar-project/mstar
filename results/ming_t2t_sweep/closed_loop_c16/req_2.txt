Leaves fall to the ground,
A carpet of gold and red,
Autumn's soft embrace.

Crisp air fills my lungs,
Whispers of change in the breeze,
Nature's symphony.

Sunlight filters through,
Dancing on amber leaves,
Autumn's gentle touch.

Leaves crunch underfoot,
Memories of childhood play,
Autumn's sweet nostalgia.

Branches bare and stark,
Awaiting winter's cold breath,
Autumn's final bow.
