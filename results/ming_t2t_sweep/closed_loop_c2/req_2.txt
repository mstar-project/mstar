Leaves fall to the ground,
A carpet of orange and red,
Autumn's beauty found.

Crisp air fills my lungs,
As I walk through the fallen leaves,
Nature's symphony.

The trees stand tall,
Their branches bare and empty,
Waiting for spring's call.

A gentle breeze blows,
Carrying leaves on its wings,
Autumn's dance begins.

The sun sets early,
Leaving the sky a deep blue,
Autumn's end draws near.
